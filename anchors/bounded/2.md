# Hexagonal route layer (forgecraft-mcp)

- repo: jghiringhelli/forgecraft-mcp@5552913
- feature: zero direct DB/ORM calls in src/tools/* — handlers delegate to analyzers and shared services

## Why this score

Tool handlers under src/tools/ never touch a database client directly. Layer
violations are scanned by collectDbViolations and the score-2 path is
"no direct DB/ORM calls detected in route or controller files".

## What would move it up

Score 2 is the maximum for bounded. The next discipline beyond this is to
extend the bounded scan to flag transport-layer leaks (e.g. HTTP-specific
response shapes leaking into domain types).
